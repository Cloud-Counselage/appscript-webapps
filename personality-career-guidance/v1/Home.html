Personality Based Career Guidance

Assessment Instructions

Purpose of the Assessment

This assessment is designed to identify your core personality archetypes - Thinker, Leader, Entrepreneur, Creator. It's a tool for self-discovery to help you understand your natural inclinations and explore career paths where you are most likely to thrive.

Before You Begin:

  • Find a comfortable space where you can answer thoughtfully without interruption.
  • Allocate about 5-7 minutes to complete the assessment.
  • Approach each question with honesty. The more authentic your answers, the more accurate your results will be.

Tips to Answer Effectively:

  • Rate yourself on a scale of 1 to 5 for each statement:
    1 = Strongly Disagree | 2 = Disagree | 3 = Neutral | 4 = Agree | 5 = Strongly Agree
  • Don't overthink your answers; your initial gut reaction is often the most accurate.
  • There are no right or wrong answers. This is about understanding your unique self.

After You Complete:

  • You will discover your two dominant personality archetypes.
  • You'll see a full score breakdown showing how you rate across all four archetypes.
  • You will receive a detailed Career Roadmap and a list of Suggested Domains for your dominant types.

Reminder:

Your personality is a powerful asset in your career journey. Use these results not as a rigid rule, but as a guide to help you make more informed and fulfilling professional choices.
